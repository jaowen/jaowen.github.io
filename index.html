I am a postdoc at Princeton working in the group of Tom Muir. Previously, I did my PhD in physics at MIT, advised by Leonid Mirny.

My interests include:

  • chromatin and epigenetic memory
  • sensitivity and error correction in living things
  • the relation between structure and function in chemical reaction networks
  • thermodynamics of computation

As an undergraduate, I studied mathematics at the University of Cambridge.
